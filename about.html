About Snippix

Learn more about our mission and the people behind Snippix.

Snippix is a free platform dedicated to helping creatives, developers, and designers access high-quality photos and videos for personal and commercial use.

We curate content from talented photographers and videographers and make it available without cost, licensing hurdles, or watermark restrictions.

Snippix is powered by the Pexels API and is continuously evolving to improve search, categorization, and user experience.

Our mission is to empower creators by removing barriers to high-quality visuals.
